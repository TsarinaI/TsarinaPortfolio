About Me

Hey y'all!

My name is Tsarina Islam. I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. I attended Westminster Choir College in Princeton, NJ on academic scholarship to study music education, but eventually earning my Bachelor of Arts in Music. I was given the opportunity to perform with many world-renown ensembles such as the Berlin Philharmonic, the New York Philharmonic, the Spoleto Festival Orchestra, and the Philadelphia Orchestra. I taught music to PK3 through 8th grade at a school in West Philadelphia.

Currently, I'm finishing a coding course through the University of Pennsylvania and hope to become a full-stack developer. I'm well-versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MYSQL, with proficiency in various libraries such as Handlebars, Express, Cheerio, and Mongoose.
